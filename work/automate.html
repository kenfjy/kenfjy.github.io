AUTOMA-TE

Creating original cams for educational CNC machine

“Otona-no-Kagaku” (Science for Adults), is a series of magazines published by Gakken, a publisher of education materials. It’s biggest feature is the unique “scientific“ gadgets that comes along with the magazine.

For its 41st volume, Gakken chose NC plotting machine, which moves based on the cam that was set, as the gadget of the volume. The main mechanics were designed by MaywaDenki. As a part of the team, I designed and created a web application that allowed users to create an original cams that would make the machine draw anything they wanted. Created in collaboration with Ken Nakagaki.

Lacu is a wall-mounted soundproof area to converse, reflect on a problem and realise a solution by oneself.
