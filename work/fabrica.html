FABRICA

Connecting to 3D printer from anywhere anytime

Wells Fargo estimates that the 3D printing market will grow from $288M in 2012 to $5.7B in 2017, and the share of revenue created by the consumers will extend as well. Although people speak how it is a versatile machine, allowing people to create anything they can imagine, a 3D printer requires users to manipulate the machine from their personal devises, yet take hours to print one simple object.

FABRICA is a web service that allows users to manipulate and control 3D printers from online interface by connecting the printers to an original board. As a member of the development team, I programmed the original board and created the main server-side control programs.

Collaborators: Masayoshi Mitsui, Konomi Morikawa, Takumi Moriya
