KEN FUJIYOSHI

Ken is a Japanese design technologist based in London. He explores the user and social interaction that technology brings, and the role of design within these interactions. As a designer, he loves experimenting with new technologies and designing user experiences around them.

Although Ken is currently studying at the Royal College of Art, he is keen to collaborate on projects that aim to explore and challenge the potential of technology through design.
